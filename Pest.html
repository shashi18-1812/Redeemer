World class fumigation solutions for quarantine purpose

Redeemer provides fumigation service for both import & export shipment for quarantine purpose in Bulk commodities as per the guidelines of Directorate of of Plant, Protection, Quanrantine & Storage, Govt of India(NSPM-11, NSPM-12, ISPM-15-Methly Bromide & NSPM-22-Aluminimum Phosphide).
Fumigation is the process of applying chemical in gaseous state in a gas tight enclosure for sufficient time to kill the quarantine pest persent in different commodties like Pulses, Rice, Maize, Wheat, Spices, Extractions, Oil Seeds, and Timber etc. which is exported to different countries. This treatment is required for elimination of quarantine pest in accordance with the requirement of the importing country. Redeemer provides pre-shipment fumigation of export & import commodity in Bulk Liner, Chartered Vessels, Aircraft, Warehouse Fumigation, Silos etc.
